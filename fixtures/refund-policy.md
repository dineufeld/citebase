# Acme Refund Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

## Eligibility

Customers may request a full refund within **14 days** of purchase if the product has not been used in production. Subscriptions billed monthly can be cancelled anytime; the current period is not prorated after day 7.

## How to request

Email billing@acme.example with:
1. Order ID
2. Purchase date
3. Reason for refund

We respond within **2 business days**. Approved refunds arrive in 5–10 business days depending on the payment provider.

## Non-refundable items

- Professional services already delivered
- Custom implementation packages after kickoff
- Third-party marketplace fees

## Chargebacks

If a chargeback is opened before contacting support, we may pause the account until the dispute is resolved.
