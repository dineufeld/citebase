# Acme Employee Handbook (sample)

## Welcome

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook summarizes policies for full-time employees in the Product and Engineering organizations.

## Working hours

Core collaboration hours are **10:00–16:00** in your local timezone. Outside core hours, async updates are preferred. Managers should not expect responses after 19:00 local time except for severity-1 production incidents.

## Remote work

Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week**. On-site days are Tuesday and Thursday unless a team agreement says otherwise. Remote work requires a quiet space and a stable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps down.

## Time off

Full-time employees receive **25 days** of paid time off (PTO) per calendar year, accrued monthly. Unused PTO rolls over up to 5 days. Parental leave is 16 weeks paid.

## Expense policy

Team lunches under **$40 per person** do not need pre-approval. Travel requires manager approval when total estimated cost exceeds **$500**. Submit expenses within 30 days via the finance portal.

## Security basics

- Use a password manager and hardware 2FA for all company accounts.
- Do not store customer data on personal devices.
- Report suspected phishing to security@acme.example within 1 hour of discovery.
